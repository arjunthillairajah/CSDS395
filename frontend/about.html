Who are we?

Dermatology AI is a diagnostic tool created to help individuals determine is they have a skin conditon that needs immediate attention.

What do we do?

Dermatology AI can be used to identify skin cancers at their source. All you need to do is upload an image and Dermatology AI will take care of the rest. Our skin cancer tool has been trained to determine the medical urgency of you skin condition regardless of age, race, or sex.

What next?

Take a clear picture of your skin condition and upload it to our tool. We will let you know what condition you are at risk for and provide care instructions. If your skin condition requires a medical opinion, we will advise you to see a medical professional. Stop your worrying now, and trust our tool to tell you if that mole is something to be worried about.
